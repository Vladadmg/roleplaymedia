My roleplaying year

Played D&D 4th - didn't like it.

Played Dragon warriors - enjoyed it.

Played Buck Rogers XXVc - enjoyed it.

Bought Deathwatch - love it, and I'm in the middle of creating an epic Dark Heresy/Rogue Trader/Deathwatch campaign.

Playing Pathfinder - meh.

 

And in between all that I opened a gaming shop and then promptly closed it again 5 months later. Now I need to get a proper, normal job.

 

That was quite a year.
