What is the Grand OGL Wiki?

The Grand OGL Wiki is a repository of Open Game Content that was published under the Open Game License. The site was created on June 18th, 2008 because Matthew Sprange (of Mongoose Publishing) offered to provide a volunteer with access to much of the d20 content so that the open game material could be preserved online if the books went out of print or digital distribution.
