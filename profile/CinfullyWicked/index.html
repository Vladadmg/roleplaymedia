All About Me in 5 Seconds

-I'm 24
-I'm attending Trent for my B.A. in English Lit.
-I'd like to attend U of T for their Masters in Creative Writing when I'm done here.
-Professionally I'd like to write comic scripts and work in the tabletop gaming industry.
-I'm a critic/reviewer that looks at music, RPGs, books and compares and contrasts Super Sentai series to their Power Rangers counterparts at Cinfully Wicked Critiques.
