Profile Information

What games do you play?
These days it's mostly WoW but I spent 27 years working in the games industry, mainly tabletop and computer games. I was inducted in the Academy of Gaming Arts & Design Hall of Fame 1996.

I've played and/or worked on a great many RPGs, CCGs, board games, computer games. Now I write the games blog and column for Library Journal and I am one of the "gaming experts team" for American Libraries Association. I still greatly enjoy the opportunities to do gaming art and design work for the industry when it comes around.
What type of media are you involved in?
Blogging, Game Design, Writing, Art, Cartography, Player
