  • Posted by Questing GM on October 25, 2009 at 10:46am in General Discussion
  • View Discussions
Since the number of members is growing very rapidly and everyone is still in the stage of getting to know each other. I thought it would be a great idea to have a few threads that list down the URL links to our respective media.

So here I'll compile the list of blogs that everyone owns in this network so we can easily reference or search each other in the future. Just reply to this thread with;

1)name of blog
2)URL of blog
3)A short blurb (maybe about a paragraph) of what you want to say about your blog

What do you say? I'll go first.

Questing GM; A Borneon Gamer
questinggm.blogspot.com
The Questing GM hails from a small gaming tribe hidden in the mountains and jungles of Borneo. He is on a quest to search for the long lost secrets of gaming Enlightenment. Join him in his quest to discuss and learn more about gaming, GMing, news and the minority views about gaming in the east.

Stargazer's World - A Role Playing Games Blog
http://stargazersworld.com/
Stargazer’s World is a roleplaying game blog. It’s a place where I can write down my thoughts on the roleplaying industry, the community and roleplaying in general. And by roleplaying I mean good old classic pen and paper roleplaying. So don’t expect too much about MMORPGs, computer and console RPGs and the like. And don’t expect posts about LARP either. Don’t get me wrong, I have played a lot of MMOs and I wouldn’t mind trying out LARP some time, but this blog will be focussed on pen and paper roleplaying.

Games of State
Gamesofstate.net
Games of State is my little blog, just recently turning 1. It covers various role-playing bits and pieces, occasional articles on other aspects of 'geek life' and (when I have the time) I also post some recipes. It's not focused on any one system, because I have quite diverse tastes and has far too many half finished projects, including a Torchwood RPG setting, a post-Apocalypic version of Scotland and (although not posted) two different alternate World of Darkness settings (one in the 1980s in a Watchman style timeline, one in a space-opera style future).

I also need to rewrite my About page.
Updates as often as I have time and inspiration ;)

RPG Blog 2
www.rpgblog2.com
RPG Blog 2 is my second go-around with RPG blogging. It's all about gaming news, reviews, analysis, and culture. I keep a schedule of updating it regularly, with special discounts and offers for my readers along the way.

Topic-wise, I love writing about the hobby in general, interviews with RPG writers/publishers, small press games, gaming culture and customs, classic D&D, and a host of other RPGs, from the old school to the new. Most Fridays, I also host Friday Discussion, where I throw out a topic for readers to answer and discuss.
