Welcome!

Welcome to RPMN's Imagination - The Art of World Creation group, the premiere group for everything world design related and the best spot to discuss your imaginative works here on RPMN.

Feel free to create discussions on whatever aspects you want to talk about, whether it's advice on map making, creating cultures, classes and races, designing things to flesh out your world such as economies, politics and the like - that's why, as fellow worldcrafters, we are here!

Every week I will post a new topic for discussion - a thought exercise, if you will.

Now let's do what we came here for - discuss and design!
