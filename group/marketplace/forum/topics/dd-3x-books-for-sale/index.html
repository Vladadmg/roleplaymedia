D&D 3.x Books for Sale

  • Posted by Kristian on October 25, 2009 at 8:17pm in Marketplace
  • Back to Marketplace Discussions
If you're in the Atlanta area and interested in stocking up on some D&D v3.5 supplements, I have a long list of 3.x books available for sale. Most of these books are in great condition. Only one or two have some noticeable wear and tear or binding issues.

While I'd like to sell each of them for 50% of their cover prices, the sale price very is negotiable.

Any titles that have a line-through have been sold.
Update: I've added the 3.5 core rule books and the Expanded Psionics Handbook to the list (see 3.5 Core Rules below).

3.0 Core Rules

  • Player's Handbook (Cover price: $19.95; original 1st printing set with promotional price; binding a bit worn)
  • Dungeon Master's Guide (Cover price: $29.95; great condition; bought new when 3.5 was released)
  • Monster Manual (Cover price: $29.95; great condition; bought new when 3.5 was released)

3.5 Core Rules

  • Player's Handbook (two copies available)
  • Player's Handbook II (Regarded as partially Complete Warrior II with lots of new higher-level fighter feats; has some 4e concepts such as feat retraining, which is also good for fighters)
  • Dungeon Master's Guide
  • Dungeon Master's Guide II
  • Monster Manual
  • Monster Manual II
  • Fiend Folio
  • Monster Manual III
  • Monster Manual IV
  • Monster Manual V
  • Rules Compendium (Excellent, easy-to-reference rules resource; great for all of those nitty gritty rules you wish you could remember)
  • Expanded Psionics Handbook (Core book for psionics)

Monster Books

  • Draconomicon: The Book of Dragons
  • Libris Mortis: The Book of the Undead
  • Lords of Madness: The Book of Aberrations

Genre Series

  • Heroes of Battle
  • Heroes of Horror

Complete Series

  • Complete Warrior
  • Complete Divine
  • Complete Arcane
  • Complete Adventurer
  • Complete Psionic (includes ardent and psionic mantles)
  • Complete Mage (reserve feats = 4e at-will powers)
  • Complete Scoundrel (skill tricks as a new character option)

Race Series

  • Races of Stone
  • Races of Destiny
  • Races of the Wild
  • Races of the Dragon (great when combined with Dragon Magic and Draconomicon)

Mature Series

  • Book of Vile Darkness
  • Book of Exalted Deeds

Equipment and Magic Items

  • Arms & Equipment Guide (lots of awesome mundane equipment that got forgotten in v3.5; includes rules for vehicles and getting drunk)
  • Magic Item Compendium (compiled and recalibrated magic items; adds new magic )
  • Weapons of Legacy (combine the custom legacy item creation rules with the weapon and armor properties from Magic Item Compendium and you get some really great stuff!)

Miscellaneous

  • Crime and Punishment (Published by Atlas Games; written by Eberron creator Keith Baker; an awesome book for anyone wanting to play inquisitives, bounty hunters, etc. or for DM's looking to run a campaign in noir campaign in Sharn; great 'magitech')
  • Dragon Compendium (Compilation of content from Dragon Magazine, published by Paizo; 100% official D&D v3.5)
  • Dragon Magic (Awesome addition to Draconomicon and Races of the Dragon)
  • Manual of the Planes
  • Psionics Handbook (Cover price: $26.95; This is the original 3e version, NOT the v3.5 Expanded Psionics Handbook; will take best offer)
  • Spell Compendium
