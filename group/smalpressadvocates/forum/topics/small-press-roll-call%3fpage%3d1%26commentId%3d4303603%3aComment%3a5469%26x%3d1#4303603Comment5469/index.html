Huzzah!

Precis Intermedia offers an eclectic mix of mostly low-cost and easy-to-learn roleplaying and miniatures games. genreDiversion titles (HardNova 2, Coyote Trail, EarthAD 2, Mean Streets, and Ghostories) give you ready-to-go PDF games for under $5. Two-Fisted tales offers definitive pulp roleplaying. Warcosm pits fleets of starships against each other. New World Disorder pits killer gangs against each other. Disposable Heroes are the leading paper miniatures, available in print and as downloads. Active Exploits Diceless is a free downloadable rules system, with add-on setting books. Brutes is a fantasy miniatures microgame for under $3 (or free with any physical order). And that's just for starters. Plus, just about all PDFs can be printed to order at purchase or at a later time.

Current projects include:
Treasure Awaits! for retro but not clone dungeon-crawling.
Bold & Brave, the supers resource for genreDiversion 3E.
Warcosm Assault, the game of Starship Boarding Actions.
GnomeMurdered, the second edition of the world's simplest roleplaying game by the RPGPundit.

Precis Intermedia also processes sales for our friends at Better Mousetrap Games, Deep7, WordMill, Arion Games, and others.

Permalink Reply by WJ MacGuffin on October 29, 2009 at 3:25pm
WJ MacGuffin here of Happy Bishop Games. I used to write stuff for Paranoia (Mongoose edition) under the pen name "Bill O'Dea" before striking out on my own. (Mostly because Mongoose fired my ass to save money.) Thanks to Zachary for starting this thread!

Happy Bishop Games currently has one finished product: Chrysalis Revolt, a short parlor narration rpg recently mentioned on the All Games Considered podcast. It is free! Download until my hosting plan bursts!

We have one game in serious development: Triune, a science fiction game combining hard sf and religion. It's a mix of Traveller, In Nomine, and Paranoia. It will be out once 1) we have the time to finish writing background material, 2) we download decent-enough art, and 3) I stop tinkering with the rules. I'm never finished! Details can be found on our website.

We have three other games in occasional development. Teria combines the spirit of Paranoia with 4E GSL rules. Fugue is a Lacuna Pt. 1 mod influenced by Jungian psychology. Lastly, Schemata is Dan Brown-meets-The Matrix

Our design plan is to release rpgs that are fun but also make you think. For example, Chrysalis Revolt centers around cruelty to genetically engineered servant-creatures. You can win the game by being good to the creatures OR by being unspeakably cruel to them. Our hope is to spark thoughts or even debate about treatment of people, pets, etc. and whether you should "win" a game by being evil.

I hope to discuss, debate, and argue with y'all soon over small press topics like finding artwork, setting prices, etc. Good luck!
