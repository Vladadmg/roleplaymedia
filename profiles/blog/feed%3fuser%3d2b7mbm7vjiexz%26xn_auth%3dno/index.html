These are some notes I made today for a squad in combat.<br /> <br /> A squad is 5 people: a leader, a communications officer and 3 fighters. All can fight but many things are based on the leader. The squad works as a unit.<br /> <br /> Initiative: 18 + 1d10<br /> To Hit: 20 + 1d10<br /> Headshot: 10 + 1d10<br /> Damage: 50 + 5d6 -- can be divided as you choose between 2 targets.<br /> This is normal damage, not armor peircing.<br /> Roll for body location normally as 1 or 2 attacks.<br /> <br /> Armor: 25 in each location; includes BTM<br /> Body Points: 200; the leader is lost is the squad takes 100 points of damage.<br /> Save: 8 - each time save is failed, a squad member goes down.<br /> Damage is reduced by 10; Body Points are reduced by 40.
