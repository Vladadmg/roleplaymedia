Cyberpunk 2020 Squad Mechanics

The problem with any mass combat game is the sheer number of attacks involved. These are some notes I made today for a squad in combat. A squad is 5 people: a leader, a communications officer and 3 fighters. All can fight but many things are based on the leader. The squad works as a unit. Initiative: 18 + 1d10 To Hit: 20 + 1d10 Headshot: 10 + 1d10 Damage: 50 + 5d6 -- can be divided as you choose between 2 targets. This is normal damage, not armor peircing.
