New Members Must Now Be Approved

  • Posted by Andrew Modro on February 24, 2010 at 10:19am
  • View Andrew Modro's blog
While it's not something we wanted to do, the recent flood of spammers taking advantage of the open-door policy on new memberships has been quite a problem. For the time being, with Berin's approval, I have set RPMN to "approval" mode, meaning new members must be approved by a member of the Brute Squad. Never fear, we'll approve anybody who isn't actually a spammer -- and yes, we know how to spot spammers -- but this should keep the online pharmacy spam out.
